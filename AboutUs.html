About us

Mission

Our mission is to help businesses grow and thrive in the digital world. Through a set of multiple resources and our professionals, we delivery services that suit the needs and goals of each client.

Vision

Our vision is to enable different industries to adopt latests technologies and become competitive, improving the general results and work environment.

History

TechCo was born in 2018, from the vision of two friends who shared a passion for technology and entrepreneurship. Both had experience in the digital sector, but wanted to create their own company that offered a personalized and quality service to small and medium-sized businesses.

With an initial investment of $10,000, they rented a small office and hired their first employee. They started to offer web design and digital marketing services to their first clients, who were satisfied with the results and recommended them to others.

Since then, TechCo has grown and evolved, incorporating new services and expanding its team. Today, TechCo has more than 50 employees, including designers, developers, marketing specialists and project managers. We have worked with more than 500 clients, from startups to large corporations, in different sectors and industries.

Values

At TechCo, we believe that technology is a powerful tool to improve the world. That's why, we are guided by the following values:

  • Innovation: We like to be up to date with the latest trends and innovations in the digital sector. We look for innovative and creative solutions that add value to our clients and their users.
  • Quality: We are committed to offering a quality service, meeting the deadlines, budgets and expectations of our clients. We strive to surpass ourselves and improve continuously.
  • Passion: We are passionate about what we do and enjoy our work. We put enthusiasm, energy and dedication into every project we carry out.
  • Honesty: We act with honesty, transparency and respect. We are clear and direct with our clients, collaborators and suppliers. We fulfill our word and our commitments.
  • Responsibility: We are aware of the impact that our work has on society and the environment. That's why, we act with responsibility and ethics, following the principles of sustainability and corporate social responsibility.
